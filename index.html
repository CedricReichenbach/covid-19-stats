This page shows stats related to the COVID-19 outbreak, comparing recorded fatalities with the number of confirmed cases. Numbers are based on data from CSSE at Johns Hopkins University.

* Note that the ratio of deaths per confirmed cases is not the same as mortality rate. There are a few factors skewing this metric:

  • Confirmed cases are just a lower bound of the number of actual cases, since many may go undetected.
  • Death cases correspond infections that happened earlier.
